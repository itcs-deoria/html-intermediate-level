~ HTML tags are generally classified 
   into two main types:

~ Opening Tag & Closing Tag: 
    Tags that come in pairs (e.g., < div>< /div>).

~ Self-Closing Tags: 
    Tags that do not require a closing counterpart (e.g., < img />, < br />).
